Quantum Intelligence Workshop (QIW 2025): Quantum Computing, Communications, and Machine Learning for Next-Gen Intelligent Systems

Workshop Description

The Quantum Intelligence Workshop (QIW 2025) is a premier interdisciplinary forum designed to unite leading researchers, academicians, and industry pioneers. Our mission is to explore the profound synergy between Quantum Computing, Quantum Communications, and Quantum Machine Learning (QML). As classical computing paradigms approach their fundamental limits, particularly in solving complex, large-scale problems, quantum technologies are poised to unlock exponential advancements in computational power and security.

This workshop will delve into the critical facets of this technological revolution, addressing the theoretical and practical challenges of building the next generation of intelligent systems. We will explore how quantum phenomena, such as superposition and entanglement, can be harnessed to create systems capable of solving problems currently intractable for even the most powerful supercomputers. The core of "Quantum Intelligence" lies in this fusionâ€”leveraging the computational prowess of quantum mechanics to fuel the cognitive architectures of machine learning.

Key areas of focus include:

  • Exploration of novel quantum algorithms for optimization, simulation, and search problems.
  • Development and analysis of secure quantum communication protocols, including Quantum Key Distribution (QKD).
  • Investigation of hybrid classical-quantum models for machine learning and AI acceleration.
  • Strategies for quantum error correction and the design of noise-resilient quantum systems.
  • Emerging use-cases and applications in post-quantum cryptography, network security, and drug discovery.

History of the Workshop

First Edition. While QIW 2025 marks the inaugural edition of this workshop, the organizing committee brings extensive collective experience from successfully chairing and managing numerous international conferences, workshops, and special sessions within the domains of computer science, networking, and security.

Call for Papers (CFP)

We invite submissions of high-quality, original research papers for the inaugural QIW 2025.

  • Quantum Algorithms and Computing:
    • Quantum-inspired and quantum-native algorithms
    • Variational quantum circuits and their applications
    • Quantum error correction and fault-tolerant computing
    • Quantum optimization for network routing and resource allocation
  • Quantum Communications and Security:
    • Quantum Key Distribution (QKD) protocols and networks
    • Post-Quantum Cryptography (PQC) and its integration
    • Federated learning with quantum-secured communications
    • Design and evaluation of quantum network devices and testbeds
  • Quantum Machine Learning (QML):
    • Quantum neural networks and hybrid quantum-classical systems
    • Applications of QML in healthcare, finance, and security
    • Quantum-enhanced AI for next-generation communication networks
    • Congestion control and resource management in quantum networks
